know more about our Rector

    He was born on 1st January, 1970 at Ilaro. The young Abiodun attended Awe High School, Awe, Oyo State between 1979 and 1984. He proceeded to the then Ogun State University, now Olabisi Onabanjo University, Ago-Iwoye for the pursuit of Bachelor of Art Degree in English and graduate in the year 1995. The Quest for more knowledge led him to register for Master Degree programme in International Relations at Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile-Ife and graduated in the year 1998.

The vision of becoming a great scholar could not allow Abiodun Oluseye to rest on his oars as he enrolled for another Master degree program in English at the prestigious University of Lagos and graduated in 2002. Having secured lecturing appointment with the Federal Polytechnic, Ilaro 1999, Oluseye resolved to pursue the career to the level of Doctor of Philosophy, this led to filling in for third degree programme at Covenant University where he bagged Ph.D in English in 2021.

Yewa Awards

     Abiodun Oluseye is a thorough bred professional who is a Fellow of Institute of Business Diplomacy and Financial Management (FBDFM) and Ife Institute of Advance Studies (IIAS). He is in active member of many other professional bodies among which are International Association of Teachers of English as second Language (IATEFL), International Association of Media and Communication Research (IAMCR), Canadian Association for Commonwealth Literature and Language Studies, Association for the Study of Literature and Environment (ASLE), African Literature Association (ALA), Lagos Studies Association (LSA), and African Studies Association (ASA) just to mention a few.

    He has initiated Community Development progress by way of championing Electrification projects at different communities, majorly at Ado-Odo-Ota Local Government and Yewa South Local Government areas. His developmental drive has granted him TETFund sponsorship in many of his research works. Some of his projects sponsored by Tetfund include Investigation of Entrepreneurship Intention among Entrepreneurship students in South-West Nigeria and change Leadership and Institutions in South-West Nigeria. The Academic titan started his career as a Lecturer III in 1999 at Federal Polytechnic, Ilaro and rose through the rank to the post of a Chief Lecturer in 2017.

his excellence

    Dr. Oluseye, is an administrator par excellence, his role as Dean of School, Chairman/Member of different Committee, and groups made him unique at his place of work to the Management, his colleagues and students populace. Abiodun’s passion for growth and development of coming generations is second to none as his students never stop calling since the assumption of office as Rector at Ogun State Institute of Technology, Igbesa. The cordial relationship built with staff and all people around him is a pointer to his progress.

Comrade Abiodun Oluseye served as Secretary and Chairman of the Academic Staff Union of Polytechnics, Federal Polytechnic Ilaro Chapter, his tenure had uninterrupted academic sessions, building of Academic Staff Union of Polytechnic (ASUP) house, establishment of soft loan facility for members of staff, initiating payment of promotion arrears among others.

His Appointment

    The pedigree built over the years has earned Dr. Abiodun Oluseye the appointment as the Third substantive Rector of Ogun State Institute of Technology, Igbesa. On assumption of office, Oluseye has turned things around for better with both students and members of Staff of the Institute. The performance of the erudite scholar in less than a year in office is a signal to the assurance of a better and greater future for the Institute Community.
